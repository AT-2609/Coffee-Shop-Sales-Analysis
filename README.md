# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Developed an interactive **Power BI dashboard** to analyze sales performance for a coffee shop by integrating data from MySQL, Excel, and Power BI. The project provides actionable insights into sales trends, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and store-level operations, enabling data-driven decision-making to improve overall business performance.

---

## 📋 Problem Statement

The coffee shop aims to gain a deeper understanding of its sales performance across multiple business dimensions. The primary objectives of this analysis include:

* Total Sales Analysis
* Total Orders Analysis
* Total Quantity Sold Analysis
* Sales Analysis by Weekdays and Weekends
* Sales Analysis by Store Location
* Daily Sales Analysis with Average Sales Benchmark
* Sales Analysis by Product Category
* Top 10 Products by Sales
* Sales Analysis by Days and Hours

The objective is to identify key sales drivers, uncover performance trends, and provide recommendations to optimize business operations.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* **MySQL** – Data storage and SQL querying
* **Microsoft Excel** – Initial data cleaning and preparation
* **Power BI** – Data modeling, visualization, dashboard development, and exploratory data analysis (EDA)

---

## 📂 Data Sources

### MySQL Database

Transactional sales data containing:

* Order ID
* Sales Amount
* Quantity Sold
* Product Information
* Store Location
* Date & Time
* Additional transaction details

### Excel Files

Used for preliminary data cleaning, validation, and preparation before importing the data into Power BI.

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning and Analysis

### Excel

* Standardized data types for numerical and date fields.
* Handled missing values and inconsistent records.
* Formatted datasets to maintain data integrity and consistency.
* Standardized product names, store locations, and date formats.

### Power BI

* Performed additional data transformation using Power Query.
* Removed duplicate records and filtered irrelevant data.
* Built a Date Table and established relationships with the transaction table for efficient time-based analysis.
* Applied conditional formatting and data validation to improve dashboard readability and accuracy.

### MySQL & DAX

Developed SQL queries and DAX measures to calculate and analyze:

* Total Sales
* Total Orders
* Total Quantity Sold
* Weekday vs Weekend Sales
* Store-wise Sales
* Daily Sales with Average Sales
* Product Category Performance
* Top 10 Products by Sales
* Sales Trends by Days and Hours

---

## 📊 Visualizations Used in Power BI

The interactive dashboard includes:

* KPI Cards for Total Sales, Orders, and Quantity Sold
* Line Charts to visualize sales trends over time
* Bar Charts for product category and store-wise analysis
* Pie Charts comparing weekday and weekend sales
* Column Charts with average sales reference lines
* Matrix Visuals for day-wise and hour-wise sales analysis
* Calendar Heatmap for monthly and daily sales exploration
* Interactive Tooltips and Dynamic Filters to enhance user experience

---

## 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

The analysis addresses the following business questions:

* How do total sales change over different time periods?
* How do total orders and quantities sold vary over time?
* What purchasing patterns exist between weekdays and weekends?
* Which store locations generate the highest sales?
* How do daily sales compare with average sales performance?
* Which product categories contribute the most revenue?
* What are the top 10 best-selling products?
* How do sales vary across different days of the week and hours of the day?

---

## 📈 Results

The dashboard successfully provides a comprehensive view of the coffee shop's sales performance by consolidating key business metrics into an interactive reporting solution. It enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, identify sales trends, evaluate product and store performance, and make informed business decisions.

## 💡 Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations were identified:

* Prioritize high-performing products while improving or replacing underperforming offerings.
* Implement targeted marketing strategies for stores with lower sales performance.
* Design weekday and weekend promotions based on customer purchasing behavior.
* Optimize staffing and promotional campaigns during peak sales hours.
* Leverage product performance insights to better align inventory and customer preferences.

---

## ✅ Conclusion

The Coffee Shop Sales Analysis dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business intelligence. Through interactive visualizations and comprehensive sales analysis, the project enables data-driven decision-making, helping optimize product strategy, operational efficiency, and overall business performance.
